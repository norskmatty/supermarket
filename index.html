What is this?

On the show "Supermarket Sweep", 90s people were challenged to shop for groceries as quickly as possible. Now you can too!

Create your list by entering each item you want into the text field.

You can delete items by clicking the cancel icon, and marked them as acquired by clicking the cart icon.

Once your list is complete, start the timer and get all your items as fast as possible. Challenge yourself to get your items as fast as you can!

Be sure to take some care around other shoppers, they aren't in on the game. Please don't hurt them.
